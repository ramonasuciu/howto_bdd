Yes, when the software product has emerged from a shared collaboration

How would this work exactly?

  1. Our PO has an idea about a new feature.
  2. He/she takes a note of that idea in our backlog.
  3. Ok, we've seen the new epic/story, but the enginerring team is not exactly sure what to do at this point.
  4. We gather the team (PO, developers, testers, UX) and discuss:
    • Why do we need this new feature?
    • What is the business value brought by the new feature?

Let's go on...

Ok, now we have defined the behavior. What's next?

  1. We have a clear story in our backlog, with predefined examples.
  2. UX starts working on wireframes, closely followed by designers.
  3. Tech leads and developers start working on the arhitecture.
  4. Developers start working on implemeting the feature.
  5. Testers start defining and automating the executable specifications.
